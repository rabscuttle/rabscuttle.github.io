Data


Our data contains the merged information from two datasets available on kaggle.com. The first dataset consists of 101 animals from a zoo. There are 16 variables that describe the animals and seven class types (Mammal, Bird, Reptile, Fish, Amphibian, Bug and Invertebrate). The purpose of this dataset is to be able to predict the classification of the animals, based upon the variables.

Our second dataset also came from kaggle.com. Since the first dataset only provided 101 rows of data, an extended dataset was created. This dataset has an additional 43 animals from several classes other than Mammal and Birds, as well as 70 animals from species maintained by the SÃ£o Paulo Zoo, which has animals of the Brazilian fauna in danger of extinction.
